Thank you so much for helping us raise money for Black Organizing Project, Planting Justice, and the State of Black Education. Action does not end now. Here are resources and ways for you to stay engaged with the Black Lives Matter movement and related social justice initiatives.

WatchLearn more about the experiences of Black students in Piedmont through this video by the Black Student Union.

LearnThree Miles: a podcast about a program that connects students from a public school in the country’s poorest congressional district with students from a private school that costs $43,000/year. The schools are 3 miles apart.

Read Just Mercy by Bryan Stevenson: a true story about America’s criminal justice system.

Act Volunteer with Planting Justice. Write a blog post, get interviewed, and share your stories with The State of Black Education Oakland. And here is a list of policy proposals for Piedmont specifically to combat anti-racism by the Piedmont Racial Equity Campaign.
